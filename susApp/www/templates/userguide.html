Welcome to elephant!

elephant is a sustainability app developed for LSBU staff and students (it can only be used with your @lsbu.ac.uk email address). Our nonprofit movement is for people at our university who want to give and get stuff for free.

elephant allows you to upload or preview available items FOR FREE! Please note that the App is moderated for your safety and the items are not immediately visible (until our administrator approves them). To sign up, simply register with your @lsbu.ac.uk email address and log in.

If you want to get something for free

  • Open the app to browse through the available items.
  • When you see something that you like, click on the image, title or description to find out more about the item.
  • If you are interested in the item, write a message to its owner.
  • When the item is no longer available, the owner will delete the item from the app (nothing for you to worry about!).

    • If you want to give something for free

  • Log in with your @lsbu.ac.uk email address.
  • At the bottom of the screen click on the post item button.
  • The item will now be visible to our administration for approval.
    • Should you have an issues whilst using the app, please do get in touch at hello@myelephant.xyz We hope you will enjoy using elephant and thank you for your help in building a more sustainable world!
    Best wishes from the elephant & LSBU teams
